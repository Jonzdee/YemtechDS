About Us

We Offers Quality CCTV Systems & Services

Yemtech Digital Surveillance is one of the leading Lagos City security companies

Yemtech Digital Surveillance is a Lagos based tech firm, we offer CCTV installation, repairs and sales services to clients within and outside Lagos State, Nigeria. We handle CCTV installation services for homes, businesses, churches, warehouses, offshore facilities, support vessels and any other location that requires surveillance. Yemtech Digital Surveillance is one of the leading Lagos City security companies when it comes to custom design and installation of state-of-the-art Closed Circuit Television systems (CCTV systems). Whether you are looking for an analog or IP surveillance system for your building, schools, shops, factories, homes, our experienced team can provide expert design and installation of CCTV systems in Lagos State, Nigeria. Yemtech Digital Surveillance offers a wide variety of security camera systems and digital recorders to the greater Lagos City area. We only use reputable, reliable security company brands such as Dahua, Hikvision, Hilook,more.
